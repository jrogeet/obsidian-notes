## Creating a TRUTH TABLE
1. Negation - *"NOT"* **~**  `~p`
2. Conjunction - *"AND"* **∧** `p ∧ q`
3. Disjunction - *"OR"* **∨**  `p ∨ q`
4. Implication - *"IF...THEN..."*  **→** `p → q`
5. Biconditional Statement - *"IF AND ONLY IF"*  **↔**  `p ↔ q`
